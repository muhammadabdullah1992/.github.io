About Me

Muhammad Abdullah is a Pakistan-based writer and researcher with a passion for social impact, environmental justice, and youth empowerment. His work explores the intersections of climate change, urban development, mental health, and public policy in South Asia. With bylines in leading national publications, Abdullah is known for turning complex issues into accessible, thought-provoking narratives. He is also an advocate for community-led change and has been part of several youth-led initiatives and social campaigns.
